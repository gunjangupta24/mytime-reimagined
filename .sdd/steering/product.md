# Product Overview

MyTime Reimagined is a proof-of-concept redesign of the firm's timesheet system (currently Infor CloudSuite Extensity / MyTime). It replaces the legacy enterprise UI with a modern, mobile-responsive web experience that reduces time-to-submit from ~10 minutes to under 2 minutes through smart defaults, calendar awareness, and intuitive charge code management.

## Core Capabilities

1. **Instant Timesheet Entry** — editable grid with one-tap 7h/day defaults, inline editing, and visual warnings for over/under allocation
2. **Smart Calendar Sync** — reads Outlook meetings and proposes charge code allocations, eliminating manual cross-referencing
3. **Mobile-First Experience** — card-based day view for phone entry with swipe navigation between days
4. **Hours Dashboard** — real-time visibility into hour distribution, trends across periods, and submission status
5. **Self-Service Recall** — ability to recall a submitted timesheet for corrections without emailing the admin team

## Target Use Cases

- **Bi-weekly timesheet submission** — semi-monthly (1st–15th, 16th–end) or weekly periods for US-based consultants and engineers
- **Calendar-driven allocation** — users who spend most of their day in meetings and want hours auto-mapped from their calendar
- **Leadership demo** — showcasing what a modernized timesheet experience could look like firm-wide
- **Mobile catch-up** — filling or reviewing timesheets from a phone between meetings

## Value Proposition

MyTime (Infor Extensity) is a 2005-era enterprise UI with no mobile support, no calendar awareness, no smart defaults, and no ability to recall submitted timesheets. Every pay period, thousands of professionals spend 10+ minutes manually re-entering the same charge codes, cross-referencing Outlook, and hoping they don't make a mistake they can't undo. This POC demonstrates that timesheet entry can be fast, intuitive, and even enjoyable — reducing friction and errors while giving users visibility into how they spend their time.
